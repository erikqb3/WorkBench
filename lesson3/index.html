Site Plan

Description

This site is a plannning model for TTBForecasts.com. This site's purpose is to inform viewers of the weather website's intended purpose, target audience, design features, and layout. Though the weather website is the subject of a school assignment, the planning and execution of its development will be professional; as examplified by this planning site.

Site Name

The name of this website is TTB Forecasts.com. T.T.B. is short for Teru-Teru Bozu (japanese weather dolls made by children to bring good luck in terms of the weather.) A side from the abreviation, the word 'Forecasts' should denote the fact that the website involves weather predictions.

Purpose

The purpose of this Website is to inform viewers of the current and future weather circumstances in certain locations. By use of up-to-date weather stories, geographic information, and relevant resources, viewers will be able to understand the weather activity and feel confident with such knowledge. The objective of this website is to be an easy source of information in terms of weather activity.
